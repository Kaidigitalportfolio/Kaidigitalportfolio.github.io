My Mid-Year Reflection

How broad is your friend group? Are peers easily accepted? What have you done/plan to do to improve the social atmosphere within your group of friends, grade-level, or school?

My friend group is fairly broad, and new people are accepted somewhat easily. I think I could try to make my friend group more open to everyone.

How have you contributed to our community this year? Provide examples of any leadership roles you have assumed or how you have been a role model. How have you grown as a leader/role model, or what are some ideas on how to assume these roles in the future?

This year, being at the highest grade in the school, I had to be a role model for many of the younger students, as well as my kindergarden buddies. I think in the future I should work harder to attain leadership roles in my community.

Are you consistent with completing work on time? What is hindering/helping you?

I am somewhat consistent on turning in my homework, but sometimes I can forget to bring my assignments to school or forget to turn them in. My natural inclination to procrastinate is hindering me, while my ability to work quickly helps me.
